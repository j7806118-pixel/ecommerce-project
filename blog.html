Simple Fashion Tips

Looking good isn't about expensive clothes. It's about knowing how to style them. Here are some simple fashion rules every man should follow.

Choose the Right Fit

Clothes that fit well instantly improve your appearance. Avoid clothes that are too baggy or too tight.

Stick to Neutral Colors

Colors like black, white, grey, navy and beige are easy to mix and always look stylish.

Layer Your Outfits

Adding jackets, overshirts, or blazers can make simple outfits look more fashionable.

Quality Over Quantity

It’s better to own a few high-quality pieces than many low-quality clothes.
